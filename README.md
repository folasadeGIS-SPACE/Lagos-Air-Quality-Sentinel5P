# Lagos-Air-Quality-Sentinel5P

## Overview

This repository contains the research materials, geospatial analysis workflows, and visual outputs for a study investigating urban air pollution in Lagos State, Nigeria, using Sentinel-5P satellite observations processed in Google Earth Engine.

The project applies cloud-based geospatial analytics, Geographic Information Systems (GIS), and remote sensing techniques to evaluate the spatial and temporal variability of nitrogen dioxide (NO₂) and carbon monoxide (CO) between 2018 and 2021.

---

## Research Objectives

- Assess annual and seasonal variations in NO₂ concentrations.
- Assess annual and seasonal variations in CO concentrations.
- Identify spatial patterns of urban air pollution across Lagos State.
- Generate publication-quality maps using GIS and remote sensing.
- Demonstrate the use of Google Earth Engine for environmental monitoring.
